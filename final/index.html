Games

Stream 'A'

RING TOSS :

Players must toss rings onto a set of pegs to score points. The player with the highest score wins.


SKETCH-A-THON :

We will draw a donkey on a board and mark a spot where the tail should be. Next, they will take turns While being blindfolded, they'll need to draw a tail to the donkey in the correct spot.


MOVIN' ON UP :

The participants are given a stack of cups in which one cup is of a different colour than the others and is kept at the bottom. They must take one cup from the top of the stack and move it to the bottom. The game continues in this way until the different colored cup rises to the top.


Stream 'B'

STACK ATTACK :

The participants have to stack the given cups in a pyramid form, and then they should reverse stack them back to the starting point without collapsing it . Participants should stack with one hand only.


BLOW UP :

The participants have to transfer the cup from one position to another position by blowing a balloon


SLING SHOT :

Participants will be given a set of cans that they stack on top of each other in a pyramid-like formation. Once the cans are stacked, players take turns using rubber bands to shoot at the cans and try to knock them down.

About Us
